Research

Research projects combining statistical modeling, machine learning, and data visualization to drive insights across diverse domains including economics, healthcare, and public policy.

US Interest Rate Prediction Model

Time series analysis project focused on predicting US interest rates over eight quarters, employing advanced forecasting techniques to model economic trends and monetary policy impacts.

Wearable Technology Market Analysis

In-depth case study of the wearable technology market, analyzing consumer behavior, market trends, and product performance metrics.

Housing Price Analysis in Pierce County

A multi-variable linear regression analysis investigating key factors influencing housing prices in Pierce County, providing insights into market dynamics and price determinants.

Health Impact Analysis of Alcohol Consumption

This project explores the relationship between weekly alcohol consumption and its lifetime health impact using data from the 1989 National Alcohol and Drug Survey. Models include MLE, Bootstrap CI, Bayesian CI, and linear regression to estimate mean drinks and evaluate risk severity.

Advanced Data Analysis and Modeling

Comprehensive data analysis project implementing advanced statistical methods and machine learning techniques to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets.

Election Outcome Prediction Model 2025

A logistic regression model was built to predict the probability of Canadians voting for the Liberal Party in the 2025 federal election, using survey and census data from 2017–2019. Predictor variables included age, education, income, and province, with post-stratification applied to improve estimation accuracy.
